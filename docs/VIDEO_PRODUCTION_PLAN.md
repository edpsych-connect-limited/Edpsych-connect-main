## 3. The Hybrid Impact Strategy: Videos vs. Screenshots

You are absolutely right—videos are for **narrative**, screenshots are for **proof**.

*   **The "Hook" (Video):** Use Dr. Scott to explain the *problem* and the *vision*.
*   **The "Proof" (Screenshot):** Immediately follow with a high-res, annotated screenshot of the Dashboard showing exactly what Dr. Scott just described.
*   **The "How-To" (Video):** Use Sarah to walk through a workflow.
*   **The "Validation" (Interactive/Screenshot):** Allow the user to click a "View Live Demo Data" button (or see a static report example).

**Strategic Placement Map:**
1.  **Hero Section:** Dr. Scott (Video) - "The Maze Solved".
2.  **Feature Grid:** Annotated Screenshots (Static) - Instant visual confirmation of features.
3.  **Deep Dives:** Sarah (Video) - "Watch how to create an EHCP in 3 minutes".
4.  **Trust/Security:** Adrian (Video) - "Your data is yours".

### Script 1: The Platform Introduction (The "Maze")
**Presenter:** Dr. Scott I-Patrick
**Setting:** Clean, modern office background or "The Golden Thread" abstract background.
**Duration:** ~90 seconds
**Voice Direction:** *Warm, empathetic, but urgent. Use pauses (...) for effect. Do not sound robotic.*

"If you're watching this... you already know the truth. The system isn't just broken—it's a *maze*. A fragmented, chaotic maze of emails, spreadsheets, and missed deadlines. And who gets lost in that maze? ...The children."

**(1:00) The "Safety Net" (The Tech):**
