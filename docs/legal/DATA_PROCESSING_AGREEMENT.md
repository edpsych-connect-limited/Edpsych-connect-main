# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)

**Between:**
1. **The Customer** (Data Controller)
2. **EdPsych Connect Limited** (Data Processor)

## 1. Definitions
Terms such as "Personal Data", "Processing", "Controller", and "Processor" shall have the meanings given in the UK GDPR.

## 2. Subject Matter and Duration
The subject matter of the processing is the provision of the EdPsych Connect World platform services. The duration is the term of the Service Agreement.

## 3. Nature and Purpose of Processing
Processing of student, staff, and parent data to facilitate educational psychology assessments, interventions, and EHCP management.

## 4. Categories of Data Subjects
Students, Parents, Teachers, Educational Psychologists, Local Authority Staff.

## 5. Types of Personal Data
Identity data, Contact data, Special Category Data (Health/SEND information), Educational records.

## 6. Obligations of the Processor
- Process data only on written instructions.
- Ensure persons authorized to process data have committed to confidentiality.
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures.
- Assist the Controller in responding to data subject rights.
- Delete or return all personal data at the end of the provision of services.

## 7. Subprocessors
The Controller authorizes the use of the following subprocessors: Vercel, Neon, Stripe, SendGrid.

## 8. Security
The Processor shall implement measures including encryption, pseudonymization, and regular testing of security effectiveness.
