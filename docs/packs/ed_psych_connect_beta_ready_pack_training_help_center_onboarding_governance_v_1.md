## 8) UX Consistency — Branding & UI guidelines

**Folder:** `docs/BRAND/`

### `brand-guide.md`

- Tone: supportive, clear, calm.
- Color: accessible palette with minimum 4.5:1 contrast.
- Typography: large x‑height, dyslexia‑friendly option.
- Motion: subtle; respect reduced motion.

### `ui-copy-style.md`

- Plain language; no AI jargon; action‑first labels; inclusive phrasing; error messages with next steps.
