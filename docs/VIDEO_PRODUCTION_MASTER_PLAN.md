# EdPsych Connect World - Master Video Production Plan

## Overview
This document outlines ALL videos needed for the platform to be beta-ready.
All videos feature enthusiastic, warm presenters using British English.
Scripts use storytelling, analogies, and conversational tone - NO robotic recitation.

---

## CATEGORY 1: LANDING PAGE / MARKETING VIDEOS

### 1.1 Platform Introduction (Onboarding Welcome)
- **Duration**: 2 minutes
- **Purpose**: First thing new users see - must be compelling
- **Tone**: Warm, passionate, inviting
- **Key Messages**: 
  - What EdPsych Connect World is
  - Why it matters
  - The transformation it brings

### 1.2 Data Autonomy & Trust
- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
- **Purpose**: "See The Magic" section - explain BYOD/data security
- **Tone**: Reassuring, confident, trustworthy
- **Key Analogy**: "Your data is like your family photos - precious and private"

### 1.3 No Child Left Behind  
- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
- **Purpose**: "See The Magic" section - explain differentiation engine
- **Tone**: Passionate, hopeful, transformative
- **Key Analogy**: "Like a master tailor fitting 30 suits from one pattern"

### 1.4 Gamification Integrity
- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
- **Purpose**: "See The Magic" section - explain Battle Royale learning
- **Tone**: Exciting, fun but professional
- **Key Analogy**: "The broccoli hidden in the chocolate cake"
