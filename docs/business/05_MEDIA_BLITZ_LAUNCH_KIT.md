### Mission
To establish EdPsych Connect as the UK's leading EdTech platform for Educational Psychologists and SEND professionals, generating 500+ sign-ups in the first 30 days.

1. **Liberation:** "Reclaim 10+ hours per week from admin"
2. **Innovation:** "The first platform built BY an EP, FOR EPs"
3. **Evidence:** "535 research-backed interventions at your fingertips"
4. **Future:** "Where psychology meets AI—ethically"

### Target Audiences
1. **Primary:** Independent Educational Psychologists (3,500 UK)
2. **Secondary:** SENCOs and SEND leaders (24,000 schools)
3. **Tertiary:** Local Authority EP services (152 LAs)
