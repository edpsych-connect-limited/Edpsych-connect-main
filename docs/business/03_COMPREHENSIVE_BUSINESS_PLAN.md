# 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## Business Overview

**EdPsych Connect Ltd** is a UK-based educational technology company providing a comprehensive digital platform for Educational Psychologists (EPs), schools, Local Authorities (LAs), and educational support services. The platform revolutionises how special educational needs (SEN/SEND) support is delivered, assessed, and managed across the United Kingdom.

## The Opportunity

The UK SEND sector faces critical challenges:
- **Staff shortages:** 40% EP vacancy rate nationally
- **Rising demand:** 77% increase in EHCPs since 2015
- **Inefficiency:** EPs spend 60%+ time on administration vs. child-facing work
- **Data fragmentation:** Information scattered across multiple systems
- **Compliance burden:** Strict statutory timelines (20-week EHCP process)

EdPsych Connect addresses these challenges with an integrated platform combining case management, assessment tools, intervention strategies, training, and AI-assisted workflows.

## Unique Value Proposition

| Feature | Competitive Advantage |
|---------|----------------------|
| **ECCA Framework** | Proprietary copyright-safe cognitive assessment methodology |
| **535+ Interventions** | Largest evidence-based intervention library in UK EdTech |
| **BYOD Architecture** | Only platform offering full data sovereignty to LAs |
| **AI Integration** | Report writing, analysis, and recommendations in minutes |
| **Gamification** | Unique student engagement through educational gaming |
