## Executive Summary

**VERDICT: Current pricing is significantly undervalued.**

After comprehensive analysis, our current pricing is approximately **60-70% below market rate** for the value delivered. While aggressive pricing can aid market penetration, the current structure risks:
- Unsustainable unit economics
- Perceived lack of quality (too cheap = suspicious)
- Inability to fund required support infrastructure

**Recommendation:** Implement tiered pricing increase of 40-60% whilst maintaining value proposition.

## 8. Implementation Strategy

### Phased Price Increase Plan

#### Phase 1: New Customers (Immediate)
- All new signups at revised pricing
- No impact on existing users
- A/B test conversion rates

#### Phase 2: Grandfathering (Month 2-3)
- Existing users locked at current rates for 12 months
- Communicate "Early Adopter Lifetime Discount" (20% off new rates)
- Builds loyalty and goodwill

#### Phase 3: Transition (Month 4-12)
- Gradual communication to existing users
- Offer annual commitment at 10% below new monthly rates
- Provide clear value justification

#### Phase 4: Full Pricing (Month 13+)
- All users on new pricing structure
- Early adopters retain 20% discount
- Regular annual price reviews
