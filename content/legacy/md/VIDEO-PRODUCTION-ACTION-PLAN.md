### Recording Workflow (Per Video)

**Before Recording**:
1. Open video script: `INSTRUCTION-FILES/VIDEO_TUTORIAL_SCRIPTS.md`
2. Find the video you're recording (e.g., Video 1)
3. Read through entire script once (familiarize)
4. Open platform in browser, login as appropriate role
5. Set browser zoom to 125% (makes UI easier to see)
6. Close all other apps and tabs (clean screen)
7. Start OBS recording

**During Recording**:
1. **Screen Recording**: Follow script step-by-step
   - Click through platform as described
   - Show each feature mentioned
   - Go slow enough to follow (not rushed)
   - Don't worry about mistakes - editor will fix

2. **Voiceover** (Two Options):

   **Option A: Record voiceover simultaneously** (Easier)
   - Read script out loud while recording screen
   - Speak clearly into microphone
   - Follow script word-for-word
   - Don't worry about perfect delivery

   **Option B: Record voiceover separately** (More control)
   - Record screen first (no voice)
   - Then record voice separately, watching video
   - Editor syncs them later

**After Recording**:
1. Stop OBS recording
2. Find recorded file (usually in Videos folder)
3. Rename: `Video01-TITLE.mp4` (e.g., `Video01-Platform-Overview.mp4`)
4. Quick watch (make sure file isn't corrupted)
5. Move to folder: `C:\Users\scott\Desktop\package\video-recordings\`
6. Move to next video

**File Naming Convention**:
```
Video01-Platform-Overview-screen.mp4
Video01-Platform-Overview-audio.wav  (if separate)
Video02-Teacher-Dashboard-screen.mp4
Video02-Teacher-Dashboard-audio.wav
... etc
```

### Recording Tips

**Screen Recording**:
- ✅ Close all notifications (Windows, browser)
- ✅ Use browser zoom 125% (easier to see)
- ✅ Move mouse slowly (easy to follow)
- ✅ Pause 2 seconds on important screens (gives viewer time)
- ✅ Highlight important buttons/fields with mouse circle
- ✅ Don't worry about mistakes - editor will cut them

**Voiceover Recording**:
- ✅ Sit 6-8 inches from microphone
- ✅ Speak clearly, moderate pace
- ✅ Add enthusiasm (you're excited about this!)
- ✅ Read script word-for-word (scripts are well-written)
- ✅ Pause 2 seconds between sections
- ✅ If you make mistake, pause, then restart sentence
- ✅ Record in morning (voice is clearer)

**Common Mistakes to Avoid**:
- ❌ Speaking too fast (editor can't fix this)
- ❌ Mouse moving too fast (hard to follow)
- ❌ Not pausing on important screens
- ❌ Recording with background noise (close windows!)
- ❌ Low microphone volume (test first!)
