## 💰 Production Options & Costs

### Option A: DIY Production (Lowest Cost)

**Tools Needed**:
- Screen recorder: OBS Studio (FREE) or Camtasia ($300 one-time)
- Video editor: DaVinci Resolve (FREE) or Adobe Premiere Pro ($55/month)
- Microphone: Blue Yeti USB mic ($130) or Rode NT-USB ($169)
- Teleprompter app: PromptSmart Pro ($30)

**Total Investment**: £300-500 one-time + £55/month (if using Adobe)

**Pros**:
- Full control over content
- Easy to update/revise
- Authentic voice (Dr. Scott explaining his platform)
- Can iterate quickly

**Cons**:
- Takes 4-6 weeks of focused time
- Learning curve for editing software
- May not look as polished initially

**Best For**: Startups, MVPs, agile updates
