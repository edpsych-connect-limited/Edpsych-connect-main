# EdPsych Connect: LA EHCP Support Portal
## Video Script & Storyboard

**Duration:** 8-10 minutes  
**Target Audience:** LA Commissioners, SEND Service Managers, IT Teams, Caseworkers  
**Tone:** Professional, reassuring, educational  
**Narrator:** Dr. Scott (founder) or professional voiceover

---

## OPENING SEQUENCE (0:00 - 0:45)

### Visual
- Aerial shot of diverse UK schools
- Transition to stressed caseworker at desk with paper files
- Statistics appearing: "20-week deadline", "Multiple professionals", "Complex coordination"

### Script
> "Every Local Authority in England faces the same challenge: coordinating EHCP assessments within the statutory 20-week timeline while ensuring every child receives the comprehensive support they need.
>
> Currently, this means juggling emails, chasing reports, manually collating contributions from educational psychologists, speech therapists, occupational therapists, and schools—all while the clock is ticking.
>
> EdPsych Connect's LA EHCP Support Portal changes everything."

---

## SECTION 1: THE CHALLENGE (0:45 - 2:00)

### Visual
- Split screen showing:
  - Left: Email inbox overflowing
  - Right: Paper EHCP files stacked
- Animated timeline showing 20 weeks shrinking
- Icons representing different professionals scattered

### Script
> "The EHCP assessment process involves coordination across multiple agencies and professionals. A single assessment might require contributions from:
>
> - The child's school and SENCO
> - An Educational Psychologist
> - A Speech and Language Therapist
> - An Occupational Therapist
> - Health professionals
> - Social care representatives
>
> Each professional works independently, often using different formats and timescales. The LA caseworker must chase each contribution, ensure completeness, and then manually merge everything into a coherent EHCP—all while tracking statutory deadlines.
>
> Miss a deadline, and the LA faces tribunal challenges. Rush the process, and children don't get the support they need."

>
> EdPsych Connect operates on a 'Bring Your Own Data' principle. This means your children's data—their names, their needs, their assessments—never leaves your control."

### Visual
- Animated diagram showing:
  - LA's own database (highlighted, secured)
  - EdPsych Connect platform (separate layer)
  - Arrows showing data flow remaining within LA boundary

### Script
> "Here's how it works:
>
> **Option 1: Self-Hosted** - Deploy EdPsych Connect within your own infrastructure. Your data stays on your servers, managed by your IT team, subject only to your policies.
>
> **Option 2: Dedicated Cloud** - We provision a completely isolated environment for your LA. Your data is encrypted, separated from all other authorities, and you retain full export rights at any time.
>
> **Option 3: Hybrid** - Keep sensitive data on-premise while using our cloud for processing. You define exactly what crosses the boundary."

### Visual
- Three architecture options displayed
- Checkmarks appearing next to security features:
  - ✓ Full data export anytime
  - ✓ No vendor lock-in
  - ✓ GDPR compliant
  - ✓ UK data residency
  - ✓ Your data, your rules

### Script
