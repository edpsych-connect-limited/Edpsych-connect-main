# Channelling Hyperactivity

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 18 seconds

---

## Script

Don't bottle the energy up. Channel it.
 Give them a job. Handing out books. Taking a message to the office. Wiping the board.
 
 These 'movement breaks' reset the dopamine levels.
 Incorporating movement into learning—acting out a story, pacing while reading—can improve retention significantly.
