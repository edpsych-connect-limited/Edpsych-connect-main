# Seating and Sensory

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

Where you seat an ADHD student matters.
Don't put them by the window or the door. Too many distractions.
Don't put them right at the back where they can zone out.

Front and centre is often best—the 'action zone'.
Or create a 'focus booth' they can choose to use.

Reduce visual clutter. If the walls are shouting, the child can't hear you.
