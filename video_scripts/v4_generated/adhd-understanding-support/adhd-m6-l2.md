# Impulse Control Strategies

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

Impulsivity is "Ready, Fire, Aim".
We need to build in the "Stop".

Teach the 'Stop and Think' strategy. Visualise a stop sign.
Use 'wait time' cards. The student has to hold up a card before they can answer.

And praise the waiting. "I love how you paused and thought about that answer."
Reinforce the inhibition, not just the correct answer.
