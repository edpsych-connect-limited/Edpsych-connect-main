# Self-Monitoring and Attention Training

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 30 seconds

---

## Script

SELF-MONITORING: Teach students to notice when mind wanders, use attention tracking sheets, vibrating watch reminders (Am I focused?), metacognition (What was I just thinking about?). MINDFULNESS: Short breathing exercises (not long meditation - ADHD can't!), movement-based mindfulness, sensory grounding (5-4-3-2-1 technique). ATTENTION TRAINING: Working memory games, cognitive training apps (evidence mixed - supplement, not replace accommodations), practice sustained attention in short bursts gradually increasing. BUILD on strengths - hyperfocus is ADHD superpower when interest high!
