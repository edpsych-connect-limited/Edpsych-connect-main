# Attention Cues

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

You need a signal that cuts through the noise.
A clap pattern, a chime, a call-and-response.

But don't just demand attention. Capture it.
Use novelty. Change your voice volume. Use props.

And keep the pace up. The ADHD brain disengages when the pace drags.
