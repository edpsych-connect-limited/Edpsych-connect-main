# Organisation Systems

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers, Parents

---

## Script

The ADHD backpack is often a black hole where homework goes to die.
Organisation is an executive function skill that needs explicit teaching.

Colour coding. Maths is always blue. English is always red.
One folder for 'To Do', one for 'Done'.

End the day with a 'pack up' routine. Don't just say "pack up". Guide them through it. "Check your planner. Do you have your blue book?"
