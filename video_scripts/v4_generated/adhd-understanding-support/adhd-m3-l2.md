# Impact on Academic Performance

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

You often hear: "He can focus on video games for hours, why can't he do his maths?"

This is the 'Interest-Based Nervous System'.
The ADHD brain engages automatically with things that are interesting, novel, urgent, or challenging.
It struggles to engage with things that are boring or repetitive.

It's not a choice. It's chemistry.
Video games provide a constant dopamine drip. Maths worksheets do not.

We need to make the learning engaging, gamified, or urgent to switch the brain on.
