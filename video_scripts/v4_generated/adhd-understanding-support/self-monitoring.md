# Self-Monitoring

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 26 seconds

---

## Script

We want to move from teacher-regulation to self-regulation.
 But we have to teach it.
 
 Use a random timer (like a buzzer app). When it goes off, the student asks themselves: Was I on task?
 They tick a sheet. Yes or No.
 
 This builds the 'metacognitive muscle'—the ability to observe one's own behaviour.
 Over time, they start to catch themselves drifting before you have to intervene.
