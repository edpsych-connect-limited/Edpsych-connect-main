# Inattentive Presentation: The Daydreamer

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

The inattentive student is the one staring out the window. Their brain hasn't stopped working; it's just drifted to a more interesting internal channel.

They often have 'slow processing speed'. You ask a question, and there's a long pause.
If you jump in too soon, you interrupt their retrieval process.

Wait. Give them the '10-second rule'.
Use their name *before* you give the instruction to alert the brain. "David... [pause]... open your book."
