# Parent Partnership

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 24 seconds

---

## Script

Parents of ADHD children are often exhausted and used to hearing only bad news.
 He wouldn't sit still. He lost his jumper.
 
 Change the narrative. Send a 'positive note home'.
 He had a great idea in history today.
 
 When you build a partnership, you create consistency between home and school. And consistency is the safety net for the ADHD child.
