# Seating, Layout, and Sensory Considerations

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 26 seconds

---

## Script

SEATING: Front and center (minimize distractions), near teacher (for monitoring and support), away from windows/doors, near positive role models. SENSORY: Reduce visual clutter, neutral wall colors, fidget tools permitted, movement breaks (brain breaks every 20-30 min), standing desk option, wobble cushion. STRUCTURE: Clear visual timetable, consistent routines, predictable transitions, warning before changes. REMEMBER: Environment affects ADHD brain significantly - small changes make big difference.
