# The 8 Executive Functions Affected by ADHD

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 30 seconds

---

## Script

Dr. Russell Barkley's model - ADHD primarily affects: 1) INHIBITION (impulse control), 2) WORKING MEMORY (holding information in mind), 3) EMOTIONAL REGULATION (managing emotions), 4) SELF-MOTIVATION (sustained effort), 5) PLANNING (organizing steps), 6) TIME PERCEPTION (estimating time), 7) TASK INITIATION (getting started), 8) FLEXIBILITY (shifting between tasks). These are the CEO of the brain functions - ADHD is executive function disorder. NOT about knowing what to do - it's about DOING what you know.
