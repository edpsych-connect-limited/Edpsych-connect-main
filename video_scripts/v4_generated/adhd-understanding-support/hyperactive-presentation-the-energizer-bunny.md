# Hyperactive Presentation: The Energizer Bunny

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 28 seconds

---

## Script

Hyperactivity is not just running around. It's fidgeting, tapping, talking excessively.
 It's the brain trying to stimulate itself to stay awake.
 
 Movement actually helps them focus.
 If you tell an ADHD child to sit still, their brain has to use all its energy to suppress the movement. There is no energy left for learning.
 
 Allow 'functional fidgeting'. Blu-tack, wobble cushions, doodle pads. Let them move so they can listen.
