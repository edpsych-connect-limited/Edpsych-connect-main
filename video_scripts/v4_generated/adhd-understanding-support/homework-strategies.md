# Homework Strategies

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 25 seconds

---

## Script

Homework is often the flashpoint for families. The medication has worn off, the child is exhausted.
 
 Keep it short. 10-minute bursts.
 Use a 'body double'—just sitting in the room with them while they work helps them stay on task.
 
 And focus on quality, not quantity. If they understand the concept, don't make them do 50 repetitions. That's torture for an ADHD brain.
