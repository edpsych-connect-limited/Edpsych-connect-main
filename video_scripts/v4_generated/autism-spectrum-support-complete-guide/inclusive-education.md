# Inclusive Education

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 20 seconds

---

## Script

Inclusion is not just geography. It's not just having the child in the room.
 Inclusion is belonging.
 
 It means the curriculum is accessible. The environment is sensory-safe. The social world is navigated with support.
 
 It means the child is valued for who they are, not tolerated despite who they are.
