# The Safety Net: AI Risk Prediction

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds

---

## Script

In high-stakes environments like aviation or medicine, we don't wait for a crash to investigate. We use predictive systems to prevent it. Why should children's education be any different?
 
 I'm Dr. Scott, and I want to introduce you to the 'Safety Net'.
 
 This is our proprietary risk prediction engine. It runs silently in the background of every case.
 
 It's not just checking deadlines. It's analysing patterns.
 
 It looks at engagement metrics. Is a case stalling? Is communication dropping off?
 It looks at historical data. Does this specific type of case usually hit a bottleneck at Week 12?
 It looks at context. Is the assigned officer overloaded?
 
 When it detects a risk, it doesn't just log it. It intervenes.
 
 It flags the case to a manager. It suggests a specific action: Reallocate to Officer B or Request urgent EP advice.
 
 We calculate a 'Risk Score' for every child. A score of 80% triggers an immediate review.
 
 This is how we move from reactive firefighting to proactive protection. We catch the fall before it happens.
