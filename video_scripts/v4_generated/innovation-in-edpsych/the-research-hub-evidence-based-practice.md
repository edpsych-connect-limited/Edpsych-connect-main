# The Research Hub: Evidence-Based Practice

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 50 seconds

---

## Script

EdPsych Connect isn't just a software platform. It's a living laboratory.
 
 Our 'Research Hub' allows us to validate interventions at a scale never seen before.
 
 When a school uses an intervention—say, 'Precision Teaching'—we track the impact. We measure the baseline, the fidelity of implementation, and the outcome.
 
 We aggregate this anonymised data across thousands of students.
 
 This gives us a 'Real-World Evidence' base. We can say, with statistical confidence: This intervention works best for Year 3 boys with working memory deficits.
 
 We also have an 'Ethics Submission Workflow' built in. If you want to run a study, you can submit your proposal directly through the platform.
 
 We are bridging the gap between academic research and classroom practice. We turn every school into a research partner.
