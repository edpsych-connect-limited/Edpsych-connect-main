# Battle Royale: Making Learning Thrilling

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 48 seconds

---

## Script

Who said revision has to be boring?
 
 We've taken the most popular gaming genre in the world—Battle Royale—and turned it into a learning engine.
 
 You enter the arena with 99 other students. But you don't fight with weapons. You fight with knowledge.
 
 To survive the 'Storm', you must answer questions correctly. To gain shields, you solve problems.
 
 The questions adapt to your ability. If you're in Year 5 Maths, you get Year 5 Maths questions.
 
 It creates a state of 'Flow'. The adrenaline of the game makes the brain hyper-focused. Retention rates skyrocket.
 
 We've seen students who hated maths begging to play another round.
 
 It turns the solitary act of revision into a social, competitive, and incredibly fun experience.
