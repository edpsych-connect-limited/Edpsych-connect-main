# AI Tutoring Agents: Personalised Learning

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 49 seconds

---

## Script

One-to-one tutoring is the gold standard. But it's expensive and hard to scale.
 
 Meet our AI Tutoring Agents.
 
 These aren't just chatbots. They are pedagogically trained agents that adapt to your child's learning style.
 
 If a student is a 'Visual Learner', the agent explains concepts using diagrams and metaphors.
 If they are 'Kinaesthetic', it suggests interactive tasks.
 
 It assesses the student's level in real-time. Foundation, Developing, Secure, or Mastery.
 
 If a student gets stuck, it doesn't just give the answer. It scaffolds. It gives a hint. It breaks the problem down.
 
 And it's patient. It never gets tired. It never gets frustrated.
 
 It provides a safe, judgement-free space for students to make mistakes and learn. That is the key to confidence.
