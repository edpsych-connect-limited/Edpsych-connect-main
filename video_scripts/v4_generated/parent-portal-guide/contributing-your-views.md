# Contributing Your Views

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 30 seconds

---

## Script

Section A of the EHCP is 'The Views, Interests and Aspirations of the Child and their Parents'. It's the most important section.
 
 Don't worry about sounding 'professional'. You are the expert on your child.
 
 Tell us what they love. Dinosaurs? Lego? Music? That's the hook we use to teach them.
 
 Tell us what worries you. The morning routine? The playground? The future?
 
 And tell us your dreams for them. Not just pass maths
