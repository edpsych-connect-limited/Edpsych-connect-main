# Legal Rights & Adjustments

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** SENCOs, SLT

---

## Script

Under the Equality Act, schools have a duty to make 'Reasonable Adjustments'.

This is anticipatory. You don't wait for the child to fail. You anticipate the barrier and remove it.

Allowing a child to leave class 5 minutes early to avoid the corridor crush.
Allowing a laptop for typing.
Allowing a uniform modification for sensory reasons.

These are not 'perks'. They are the levelling of the playing field. They are a legal right.
