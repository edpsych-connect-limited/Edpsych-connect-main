# The Autistic Experience: First-Person Perspectives

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 75 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers, Parents

---

## Script

If you've met one autistic person... you've met one autistic person. But there are common threads in the internal experience.

Many autistic adults describe the world as 'too much'. Too loud, too bright, too unpredictable.

Imagine walking into a supermarket, but you can hear the hum of the fridge, the scratch of your wool jumper, and the flickering of the fluorescent light all at the same volume as the person talking to you. That is the reality of sensory gating issues.

It's not that they are 'acting out'. It's that they are overwhelmed.

Understanding this internal reality is the first step to empathy. We need to move from 'behaviour management' to 'stress reduction'.
