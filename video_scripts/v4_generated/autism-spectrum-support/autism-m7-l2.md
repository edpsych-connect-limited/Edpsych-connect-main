# Promoting Independence

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers, TAs

---

## Script

We support executive function by being the external hard drive.

Break tasks down. "Get your book" is too big.
"Open your bag. Take out the blue book. Put it on the desk."

Use checklists. Use timers to show the passage of time.

But the goal is always to fade the support. We want them to use the checklist, not rely on the adult. We are building independence, not dependence.
