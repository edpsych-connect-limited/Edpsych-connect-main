# Strengths-Based Approach

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

It is exhausting to constantly work on your deficits. Imagine if your job performance review only listed the things you were bad at.

We need to identify and celebrate the strengths.
Is it visual memory? Logical reasoning? Honesty? Attention to detail?

Build the child's self-esteem around what they can do. "You are a researcher." "You are a pattern finder."

When a child feels competent, their resilience grows.
