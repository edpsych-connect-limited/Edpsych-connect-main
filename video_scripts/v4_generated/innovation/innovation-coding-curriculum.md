# Developers of Tomorrow: Inclusive Coding

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 75 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers, Parents, Students

---

## Script

Technology is the future of work. But too often, SEND students are excluded from computer science because the tools aren't accessible.

We've built the 'Developers of Tomorrow' curriculum to change that.

It's a progressive learning path. We start with 'Block Coding'—visual, drag-and-drop logic that requires no typing. Perfect for students with dyslexia or dyspraxia.

Then we bridge to Python. But we use our 'Assistive Editor'. It has predictive text, syntax highlighting, and even voice-to-code capabilities.

Finally, we introduce React—the same technology used to build this very platform.

We use 'Gamification' to keep engagement high. Students earn XP, unlock badges, and track their streaks.

But it's not just a game. It's a career pathway. We are proving that neurodivergent thinking is a superpower in coding.

Let's build the next generation of innovators.
