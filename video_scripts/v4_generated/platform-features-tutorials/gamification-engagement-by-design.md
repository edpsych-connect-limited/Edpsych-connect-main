# Gamification: Engagement by Design

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 27 seconds

---

## Script

Why do children love games? Because the feedback is instant, and the progress is visible.
 We've brought that psychology into EdPsych Connect.
 
 Meet the Leaderboards and Achievements.
 Every module completed earns XP. Every milestone unlocks a badge.
 
 This isn't just for fun. It triggers the dopamine reward pathway—the very system that needs support in ADHD.
 It turns I have to learn into I want to level up.
