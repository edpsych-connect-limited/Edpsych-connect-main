# The Local Authority Dashboard

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 75 seconds
**Audience:** LA Officers

---

## Script

For Local Authorities, the challenge is volume. Thousands of cases, tight deadlines.
Our Dashboard is your command centre.

See your statutory compliance in real-time. "98% of EHCPs within 20 weeks."
Drill down into the bottlenecks. Which schools are delaying returns? Which cases are stuck in draft?

It turns data into intelligence. You can spot a trend before it becomes a crisis.
