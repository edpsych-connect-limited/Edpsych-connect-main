# Progress Monitoring

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** SENCOs

---

## Script

How do we know if it's working? We measure.
But don't measure once a year. Measure every few weeks.

Use short, timed probes. "How many words can you read in one minute?"
Graph it. Show the student the line going up.

Success is the best motivator. When they see they are getting faster, they start to believe in themselves again.
