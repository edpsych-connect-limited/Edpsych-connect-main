# What is Reading Fluency? Accuracy, Rate, Prosody

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 40 seconds

---

## Script

FLUENCY: Reading with accuracy, appropriate rate, and prosody (expression). Critical because slow, labored reading impairs comprehension (cognitive load). Dyslexic readers often accurate but SLOW - fluency is key intervention. COMPONENTS: ACCURACY (correct word reading - prerequisite), RATE (words correct per minute - age-appropriate), PROSODY (phrasing, expression, intonation - sounds like talking). WHY DYSLEXICS STRUGGLE: Even after learning to decode, dyslexic readers don't develop automaticity easily - every word requires effort. IMPACT: Slow reading → reduced comprehension, tires quickly, reads less (less practice = less improvement), avoidance. GOAL: Automaticity - reading without conscious effort, freeing working memory for comprehension.
