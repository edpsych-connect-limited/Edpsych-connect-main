# What is Reading Fluency?

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 22 seconds

---

## Script

Fluency is the bridge between decoding and comprehension.
 It's not just speed. It's accuracy + speed + expression (prosody).
 
 If a child is using all their brain power to decode c-a-t, they have zero brain power left to understand that the cat is sitting on the mat.
 Fluency frees up the brain for meaning.
