# Comprehension Strategies

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 23 seconds

---

## Script

Dyslexic children often have high verbal intelligence but low reading comprehension. Why? Because the effort of reading drains their battery.
 
 We need to separate the two.
 Let them listen to audiobooks. Let them watch videos.
 Teach comprehension strategies orally. Predict what happens next. Summarise the story.
 
 Don't let their reading level become the ceiling for their learning.
