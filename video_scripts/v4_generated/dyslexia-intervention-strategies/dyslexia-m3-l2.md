# What is Phonological Awareness?

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

Think of Phonological Awareness as an umbrella.
Under it, you have rhyming, syllables, and the smallest unit: phonemes.

Dyslexic children often have 'fuzzy' phonological representations.
They might not hear the difference between 'pin' and 'pen'.

Our job is to sharpen the focus. We need to train the ear before we train the eye.
