# Supporting Written Expression

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 24 seconds

---

## Script

For a dyslexic student, writing is a cognitive marathon.
 They have to think of the idea, hold it in working memory, structure the sentence, recall the spelling, and form the letters.
 It's too much.
 
 Scaffold it.
 Use speech-to-text software.
 Use mind maps to plan.
 Scribe for them.
 
 Let them show you what they know, not just what they can write.
