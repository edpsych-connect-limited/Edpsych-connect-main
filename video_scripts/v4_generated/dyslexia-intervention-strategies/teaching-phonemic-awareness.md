# Teaching Phonemic Awareness

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 34 seconds

---

## Script

Phonemic awareness is the ability to hear and manipulate the individual sounds in words.
 It is the number one predictor of reading success.
 
 You can do this in the dark. It's about ears, not eyes.
 Say 'cat'. Now say it without the /c/. (at)
 Change the /a/ in 'cat' to /o/. (cot)
 
 If a child can't do this orally, they will struggle to do it with letters.
 Spend 5 minutes a day on these 'sound games'. It builds the foundation for everything else.
