# Spelling Instruction

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 70 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

Spelling is harder than reading. Reading is recognition; spelling is recall.
Don't just give a list of random words to memorise on Friday. That's a memory test, not a spelling lesson.

Teach the rules.
"Why do we double the 'p' in 'hopping'?"
"When do we use 'c' vs 'k'?"

When they understand the logic, they don't have to rely on rote memory, which is often a weak point for dyslexic learners.
