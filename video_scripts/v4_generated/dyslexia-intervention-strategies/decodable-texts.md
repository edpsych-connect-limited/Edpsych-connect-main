# Decodable Texts

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 34 seconds

---

## Script

Imagine learning to drive, and your instructor puts you in a Formula 1 car. That's what happens when we give a beginner reader a book full of words they haven't been taught.
 
 We need 'Decodable Texts'.
 These are books that only contain the sounds the child has learned.
 If they know 's, a, t, p, i, n', the book should only use those letters. Pat sat in the pan.
 
 It builds confidence. It proves to the child: I can read this.
 Success breeds motivation.
