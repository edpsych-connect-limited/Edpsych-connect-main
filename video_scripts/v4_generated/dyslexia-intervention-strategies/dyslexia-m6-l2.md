# The Simple View of Reading

**Speaker:** Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Audience:** Teachers

---

## Script

The Simple View of Reading is a formula:
Decoding x Language Comprehension = Reading Comprehension.

If Decoding is 0, Reading Comprehension is 0. Even if they are a genius.
Dyslexia is a deficit in the Decoding box.
Our job is to fix the decoding while keeping the Language Comprehension high.
