COURSE: ADHD Understanding & Support
MODULE: 7: Academic Support for ADHD Students
LESSON: 1: Organization Systems and Time Management
INSTRUCTOR: Dr. Scott Ighavongbe-Patrick (Male Avatar)
---------------------------------------------------

ORGANIZATION: ADHD executive function makes organization extremely difficult. SYSTEMS: Color-coded folders (one per subject), checklist for packing bag, homework planner (teacher checks daily), locker organization support, photograph of "organized desk" to reference. TIME MANAGEMENT: Visual timers, break tasks into timed chunks, teach time estimation (always underestimate!), alarms/reminders, prioritization strategies. TEACH EXPLICITLY: Organization isn't intuitive for ADHD - must be taught step-by-step and scaffolded.
