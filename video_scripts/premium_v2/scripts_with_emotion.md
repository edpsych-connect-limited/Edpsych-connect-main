## VOICE DIRECTION SUMMARY

### Avatar Selection
- Warm, approachable professional (NOT corporate anchor style)
- Slight expressiveness in face/gestures
- Eye contact that feels genuine

### Voice Selection
- British English (matches UK education context)
- Natural pacing with room for pauses
- Warm timbre, not broadcast-polished
- Someone you'd want to have coffee with

### Background
- Subtle, professional (soft gradient or abstract)
- NOT pure black or stark white
- Consider warm tones that feel inviting

### Avoid At All Costs
- Reading cadence (equal emphasis on each word)
- Monotone delivery
- Rushed pacing to fit time constraints
- Robotic transitions between sections
