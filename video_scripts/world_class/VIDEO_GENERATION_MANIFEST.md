### Avatar
- Use the same avatar as existing videos for brand consistency
- Warm, professional, enthusiastic tone
- British English pronunciation

### Background
- Clean, professional office or studio setting
- Subtle educational elements (books, plants)
- Match existing video style

### Pacing
- Natural conversational speed
- Strategic pauses for emphasis
- NOT robotic or rushed

### Music
- Subtle background music at 10-15% volume
- Uplifting but not distracting
- Fade out during key points
