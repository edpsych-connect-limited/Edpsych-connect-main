## Script

[WARMTH] Have you ever felt like you're drowning in paperwork while the children who need you most are waiting? 

[PAUSE]

That mountain of annual review deadlines, the tribunal anxiety, the funding calculations that never quite add up... I've been there. Every SENCO and educational psychologist I know has been there.

[THOUGHTFUL] When I first started in this field, I genuinely believed that if I just worked harder, stayed later, organized better—I'd somehow manage it all. But here's what I learned: the problem isn't you. It's the system that expects superhuman administrators while giving you paper-based tools from the 1990s.

[ENERGY] That's exactly why we built the EHCP Management Modules. Not because technology is exciting—though, honestly, it is—but because every minute you spend wrestling with spreadsheets is a minute you're not spending with a child who needs your expertise.

[PAUSE]

Let me show you what's possible.

[WARMTH] Picture this: It's September, and instead of panic-scrolling through folders trying to work out which annual reviews are due when, you open your dashboard and everything's there. Colour-coded by urgency. Linked to each child's Golden Thread. One click and you see their complete picture.

[SLOW] Seven modules. One unified system. Every piece of the EHCP puzzle connected.

**Annual Reviews** – your deadline guardian. Automated reminders, standardised templates, outcome tracking across time. Parents get their slots, you keep your sanity.

**Mediation and Tribunal** – because sometimes relationships get complicated. Track every case, every communication, every document. If SENDIST comes calling, you're ready.

**Phase Transfers** – from nursery to primary, primary to secondary, secondary to post-16. Each transition mapped, every deadline tracked, no child falls through the gaps.

**Compliance and Risk** – here's where it gets clever. Our AI doesn't just count your EHCPs; it predicts where problems might emerge before they become crises. A SENCO in Birmingham told me this feature alone saved her three tribunal cases last year.

**Resource Costing** – let's talk money, because provisions need funding. Element 1, Element 2, Element 3—calculated, tracked, justified. When the finance director asks why you need additional funding, you'll have the evidence.

**The Golden Thread** – this is the heart of it all. Needs, provisions, outcomes—all connected, all visible. When a parent asks "Is my child actually making progress?", you can show them exactly how each provision links to each outcome.

**SEN2 Returns** – annual statistical returns to the Department for Education. Automated data extraction, validation checks, one-click generation. What used to take weeks now takes minutes.

[SERIOUS] I need to be honest with you. These tools won't make difficult decisions for you. They won't have that conversation with the parent who's disappointed with their child's progress. They won't magically create hours in your day.

[PAUSE]

[ENERGY] But they will clear away the administrative fog so you can see what matters. They'll give you back the time you need to do what you actually trained for—understanding children, supporting families, building futures.

[WARMTH] In the videos that follow, we'll take you through each module in depth. Real scenarios. Practical workflows. The tips and shortcuts that transform these tools from features into your daily practice.

[THOUGHTFUL] You know, the best SENCOs I've met aren't the ones who work the longest hours. They're the ones who've figured out which work actually matters—and found ways to do less of everything else.

[SLOW] These modules are how you do less of everything else.

[PAUSE]

[ENERGY] Ready to explore? Let's start with Annual Reviews—the module that most SENCOs tell us changed everything.
